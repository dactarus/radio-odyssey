🎧 Add Radio Odyssey to Your Home Screen

On Android and iPhone, you can add Radio Odyssey directly to your home screen for one-tap listening anytime.

🍎 On iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open Radio Odyssey in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen
  4. The Radio Odyssey icon appears!
🤖 On Android (Chrome)
  1. Open Radio Odyssey in Chrome
  2. Tap Install app
  3. Confirm installation
  4. The app installs like a real app!
